## Project Overview
This project analyzes a dataset from a bank's marketing campaign. The goal was to identify patterns in customer behavior and determine the key factors that lead to a successful subscription (Term Deposit).

**Key Metric:** The campaign achieved an **11.27% Conversion Rate** (significantly higher than the industry average).

## üí° Key Business Insights
Based on the data analysis, the following conclusions were drawn:
* **Target Audience:** The highest conversion rates were found among **Students** and **Retirees**. Working-age adults (30-50) showed lower interest.
* **Engagement Matters:** There is a direct correlation between call duration and success. Successful calls averaged ~8 minutes, while failed calls averaged ~3 minutes.
* **History Repeats:** Customers who successfully subscribed in previous campaigns had a **60-70%** chance of subscribing again (Retention is key).
